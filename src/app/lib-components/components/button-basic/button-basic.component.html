Basic

The GrnButton component is a styled button.
It appears in three versions:

  • as text (exterior="text" - default)
  • as a container (exterior="contained")
  • as an outline (exterior="outlined")

The content of the grnButton component is determined by the content between its opening and closing tags. This can be a fragment of html elements that will be displayed as the content of the button. Another option, the content of the hyperlink - in this case, the hyperlink will remain. In practice, there are often situations when a button is displayed on the design, but in terms of functionality it is a hyperlink. The second option is designed for such situations.
